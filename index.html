Bio

I study intelligent solutions focusing on image analysis and robotics to aid clinical tasks. I completed my undergraduate degree in Biomedical Engineering and Master's degree in Robotics at Johns Hopkins University. If you are interested, please check out my projects or CV.

In my spare time, I enjoy various sports and dabbling in creative efforts. Feel free to check out my gallery for some visual examples.

Projects

Image-Guided Intraoperative Spinal US

Deformable 2D-3D registration to pre-operative CT scans to reduce patient and physician radiation dose. Bone is segmented using a modified nnUNet.

Vertebra Prediction

Spine segmentation and vertebra prediction from x-rays using an iterative segmentation and classification combined network.

Dislocation Detection

Dislocation detection within hips, shoulders, elbows, knees, and total arthroplasties with a pre-trained ResNet-152 fine-tuned using transfer learning.

If you are interested in learning more, please check out my Google Scholar page.
